Modified Rules

The following rules are "unofficially" used at Brisbane Swordcraft.
I will only update this page upon confirmation with the admins that a "changed" rule is actually legit, but please let me know if you hear about a new one, so I can go check it with them and get it up here ASAP.
  • Armour changes:
    If you have believable armour coverage on part of your limb, you get HP for that "zone".
    There are three "zones" for limbs: lower, joint, and upper.
    E.g: if you have bracers on your lower arms and chainmail covering your upper arms, your arm total would be +2 HP.
    Situation is unclear as to whether lopsided zones count, i.e. whether full Right Leg and lower Left Leg would be +1 or +2.
